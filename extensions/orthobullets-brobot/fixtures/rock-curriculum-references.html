Recommended Reading: Proximal Humerus Fractures

This page summarizes key references for proximal humerus fracture management and rehabilitation principles.

References

  • Neer CS. Displaced proximal humeral fractures. J Bone Joint Surg Am. 1970.
  • Hertel R, et al. Predictors of humeral head ischemia after intracapsular fracture. J Shoulder Elbow Surg. 2004.
  • Robinson CM, et al. Open reduction and internal fixation versus nonoperative treatment. J Bone Joint Surg Am. 2011.
  • Launonen AP, et al. Operative versus nonoperative treatment of proximal humerus fractures in elderly patients. J Shoulder Elbow Surg. 2015.
