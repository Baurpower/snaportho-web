Rotational Ankle Fractures

A patient has a supination-external rotation ankle fracture with medial clear space widening after stress imaging. What is the most appropriate treatment?

  1. Short leg cast
  2. Functional brace
  3. Open reduction and internal fixation
  4. Immediate arthrodesis
Medial clear space widening indicates deltoid incompetence and an unstable ankle fracture pattern. Operative fixation restores fibular length and mortise stability.
