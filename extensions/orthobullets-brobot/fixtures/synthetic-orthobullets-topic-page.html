Femoral Neck Fractures

42 Questions 11 Cards 5 Videos

Introduction

  • Fracture through the femoral neck, most common in elderly patients with osteoporotic bone.
  • Young patients with femoral neck fractures are usually high-energy trauma.

Treatment

  • Nondisplaced fractures in young patients require urgent reduction and fixation to minimize risk of avascular necrosis.
  • Displaced fractures in elderly patients are typically treated with hemiarthroplasty or total hip arthroplasty.
